Eternal Blue is a band consisting of Marius Solheim and his wife Francine Solheim. Marius studies front-end development and Francine is studying and lecturing on cyber security. The band name was taken from the cyber attack exploit EternalBlue. All the music is written and produced by Marius and Francine and is inspired by a plethora of bands and artists.
